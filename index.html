About Us

We are a team of graphic designers specialised in vector illustrations. We can create any sort of vector design, both from scratch and from a given raster picture.

Just describe us what kind of illustration you need. It may be an icon, a logo, a character - basically anything that can be designed. Or provide us your picture and we'll vectorize it for you!

*We are open-source designers, we support the free alternatives and all our works are made using Inkscape and Gimp.
